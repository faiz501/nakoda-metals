About Nakoda Metals

Crafting Excellence in Metal Solutions Since Our Inception

Your Trusted Metal Partner in Vapi

Located in the industrial hub of Vapi, Gujarat, Nakoda Metals has established itself as a premier manufacturer of brass and copper wires. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.

We specialize in producing high-grade brass and copper wires that meet international standards, serving industries across India and beyond. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility combines traditional craftsmanship with modern technology.
